Spoke or get spoked

With SPOKED you compete with your friends for two weeks to push each other into pure bike happiness. You can either start a battle or get summoned by a friend. To start your own, you need a group of 4-12 eager people.

Make snazzy patterns

Give your friends something to talk about! You battle by tracking your rides with your smartphone. SPOKED will convert the rides into snazzy patterns on the game board.

Bike for smiles

You compete for smiles. Pedal up smiles by riding miles, and by riding consistently. The contestant with the most smiles wins, and will top the battle for happiness.

Make your mom proud

Make your mother proud of your efforts in the battlefield! At the end, you can order a poster for her fridge. Or, you can have it for your own wall and relive your bike adventure for years to come.

The faces behind SPOKED

Hello! We are Kristin and Carrie, and we are on a mission to help you build bike cultures. We've been riding together for two years while studying Interaction Design at SVA in New York. With the help of Yang, our code mastermind, we are now ready to spread the bike love far and wide!

Follow us on @iamspoked, or just get off the internet and on your bike!
