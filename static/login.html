Do you want to play?

SPOKED wants to conquer the world with bike joy, and are working to get you on board! Right now our capacity is limited, and we are currently only hosting games in NYC.

Thank you!

Let the games begin

For a SPOKED game, you need to gather 4-12 eager people that are ready to fit some play into their everyday lives for two weeks. You can either start a game, get invited by a friend, or join a group of strangers.

Color the gameboard

Track your rides with your smartphone, and SPOKED will convert the rides into snazzy patterns on the map. Be creative, give people something to talk about!

Explore the gameboard

Bike for smiles

To play, join a team, and compete for smiles. Pedal up smiles by riding miles, and by riding consistently. On your third day of riding in a row, you are rewarded double for your efforts.

Make your mom proud

Make your mother proud of your beautiful patterns! At the end, you can order a poster for her fridge. Or, you can have it for your own wall and relive your bike adventure for years to come.

The faces behind SPOKED

Hello! We are Kristin and Carrie, and we are on a mission to help you build bike cultures. We've been riding together for two years while studying Interaction Design at SVA in New York. With the help of Yang, our code mastermind, we are now ready to spread the bike love far and wide!

Follow us on @iamspoked, or just get off the internet and on your bike!
