SPOKED

Battle for your happiness

We believe you are happier when you ride a bike. Dare to challenge your friends in a two-week bike game to get those wheels rolling.

We are currently made for New York and in private beta. If you have an interest in hosting a game or want to keep up to date, get in touch below.

Thank you!

Make snazzy patterns

During the game, track every ride you take. We will convert your bike rides into beautiful marks on a city map shared with your friends. To make this happen, your phones need to be smart. Both the iPhone and Android are acceptable.

Bike for smiles

Pedal up smiles by riding miles. One mile equals one smile. You get extra smiles for being loyal to your bike seat. If you ride for 3 consecutive days, your third day is a super-spoked day. You get double the smiles for your miles gained that day. The winner is the one who ramps up the most smiles!

Compare with rivals

Match your closest rivals up against you on the game board, and feel the wonderful social pressure. Compare how much they have been riding each day, and how ambitious they are on their super-spoked days!

Get the pep talk

We'll be sure to let you know when your bike has ants in its' pants and needs to hit the streets, and when it's a perfect day for riding. Right now, we talk over twitter. Follow us @iamspoked to get extra encouragement during the game.

Make your mom proud

We hope you make your mother proud throughout the two weeks of the game! So badly that, we will make a poster for her hang on the fridge to brag about. Or, you can have it for your own wall, and relive the adventure throughout the years to come!
