SPOKED

Battle for your happiness

You are happier when you ride a bike. Spoked helps you and your friends get on your bikes more often through a two week bike battle.

Thank you!

Spoke or get spoked

In SPOKED we battle for two weeks straight, and push each others limit into pure bike happiness. You can either be invited to a battle or start your own. To start your own, you need a group of 4-12 friends or co-workers.

Make snazzy patterns

During the battle, track every ride you take. SPOKED will convert your bike rides into beautiful marks on a city map shared with your friends. To make this happen, your phones need to be smart. Both the iPhone and Android are acceptable.

Bike for smiles

Pedal up smiles by riding miles. One mile equals one smile. You get extra smiles for being loyal to your bike seat. If you ride for 3 consecutive days, your third day is a super-spoked day. You get double the smiles for your miles gained that day. The winner is the one who ramps up the most smiles!

Compare with rivals

Match your closest rivals up against you on the game board, and feel the wonderful social pressure. Compare how much they have been riding each day, and how ambitious they are on their super-spoked days!

Get the pep talk

SPOKED will make sure to let you know when your bike has ants in its' pants and needs to hit the streets, and when it's a perfect day for riding. Right now, we talk to you over twitter. Follow us at @iamspoked to get extra encouragement during the game.

Make your mom proud

SPOKED wants you to make your mother proud throughout the two weeks in the battlefield! So badly that we will make a poster for her to hang on the fridge later. Or, you can have it for your own wall, and relive the adventure throughout the years to come!
