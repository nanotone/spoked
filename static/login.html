SPOKED

Battle for your happiness

We believe you are happier when you ride a bike. Dare to challenge your friends in a two-week bike game to get those wheels rolling.

We are currently made for New York and in private beta. If you have an interest in hosting a game or want to keep up to date, we'd love to hear from you!

Thank you!

Make snazzy patterns

Begin your game with a ride. But before you start pedaling, start tracking. We'll be converting the bike rides you take on the city streets into beautiful marks on a communal city map shared with your friends. Your phones need to be smart, though. Both the iPhone and Android are acceptable.

Bike for smiles
1 mile = 1 smile

Pedal up smiles by riding miles. One miles equals one smile. You get extra smiles for riding consistently. If you ride for 3 consecutive days, your third day is a super-spoked day. You get double the smiles for your miles gained that day. The winner is the one who ramps up the most smiles!

Feel wonderful social pressure

On the gameboard you can compare with your closest rivals and get up to date on your friend's progress and current bike status.

Hear your bike calling you

We'll be sure to let you know when your bike has ants in its' pants and needs to hit the streets. Since we're well into the 21st century, we talk over twitter.

Be proud. Show your mom.

We sure hope you make your mother proud throughout the two weeks of the game! We hope it so badly, that we will make you a poster that she can hang on the fridge and use to brag about you. You probably shouldn't let her down. Or just put it on your own wall to relive this two-week city adventure!

What you need

6-12 spoked people with intelligent phones. MotionX GPS for iPhone ($1) or MyTracks for Android (free). We hope to make our own app in the future, but for now these work very well with our SPOKED web interface. Here's how you set up the iPhone to get the data to us.

If you want to get really excited during the game, you probably should have a Twitter account so we can talk to you. And right now, while you're busy gathering spoked people to form a team of friends and rivals, you could also consider following @iamspoked to see what we're up to!
