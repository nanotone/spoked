Phone Setup

Download MotionX-GPS ($1) for iPhone or My Tracks (free) for Android. Then get ready for a small setup session to make sure you know how to track, and that SPOKED receives your rides.

MotionX-GPS setup for iPhone


SHARING SETTINGS

1. Open the app. Go to Menu > Setup > Share

2. Scroll down to Email share and the form below it.

3. Write your Display name: Firstname Lastname. This needs to be the very same name as the name you registered in your SPOKED account.

4. Send to: yes@iamspoked.com

5. Send to: your own email address to keep track of whether or not you have shared a ride.

6. Set Email Share switch to ON.

7. Scroll down and set One-Click share to ON. Then you can easily hit the Share button after you biked, and your data will be sent to SPOKED immediately.

8. Press Done.


TRACK RIDES

1. Open the app. Go to Menu > Record track

2. Hit Reset track if you have previously tracked a ride, to make sure you start at 00:00:00.

3. Hit Start track. This typically needs to happen when you’re outside so the phone has GPS signal – when globe is blue & green.

NOW RIDERIDERIDE!

4. Pause track when you’re at your destination.

5. Save track

6. Share track. With the click of one button the ride will be shared with SPOKED and your own email account if you provided that in the setup. Please try to share your rides immediately after you stop tracking. But if you forget, you can share a track from Menu > Tracks, or Menu > Share.


VOICE COACHING
Menu > Setup > Voice Coaching

Depending on how often you want this sexy woman to update you on your pace etc, change settings here. The 3,2,1 countdown is kinda entertaining, so leave it on if you want to feel like a rocket ready to launch every time you jump on your bike!


My Tracks setup for Android


DOWNLOAD APP AND SET EMAIL

When you download the app, it will prompt you to login with your gmail account. Be sure to use the same email here that's registered with SPOKED.

TRACK YOUR RIDE & SHARE

1. Menu Press 'Record track'.

RIDE YOUR BIKE!

2. Menu Press 'Stop recording'.

3. Press 'Save'.
You do not need to enter any Track details.

4. Press the […] button.

5. Select 'Share with friends'

6. Select 'A GPX file'

7. Select 'Mail'
Be sure to send from the email address registered with SPOKED. To change this email, press 'From: ' and select the correct email account.

8. Send email to: yes@iamspoked.com
You don’t need to change anything in the subject or body, just press 'Send'. That’s it!

Follow us on @iamspoked, or just get off the internet and on your bike!
